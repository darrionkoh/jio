# Jio! 🇸🇬

A clean and simple going-out toolkit built for Singaporeans, by Singaporeans. No gatekeeping, just helpful tools for your next gathering. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Supabase.

## Features

- **Bill Splitter** — Confirm plus chop easy to use. Add people, assign items, and auto-split with local tax logic so you don't need to end up scratching your heads.
- **GST Calculator** — Quickly calculate Singapore's 9% GST and 10% service charge. We handle the "++" so you don't need to.
- **Owe Money Pay Money (O$P$)** — Track who owes who across multiple outings. Includes "fresh" vs "old" debt badges so nobody forgets to pay back.
- **Spin The Wheel** — Everyone saying "anything" or "don't know"? Let the wheel decide your food fate based on the group's cravings.
- **Events (Jio!)** — Don't say bojio! Create events, generate unique JIO-IDs, and let your kakis join. Everything syncs in real-time via Supabase.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3
- **Backend/Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL)
- **Hosting:** Vercel

## Singapore Tax Rates & Logic

- **Service Charge:** 10% (applies to subtotal)
- **GST:** 9% (applies to subtotal + service charge)
